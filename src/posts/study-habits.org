* TL;DR
I really encourage an old-fashioned style of learning where possible. If you came to the
university to *truly learn*, then it should be no issue to pour over a dusty old book at 11:00pm
in the library.

* Being realistic
A common flaw I see in many students is an unrealistic expectation of themselves. I often see students
struggle to 'get off the ground' with their examination prep because *they try to do too much at once.*
